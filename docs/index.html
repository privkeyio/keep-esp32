Keep FROST Signer

Air-gapped threshold signing for ESP32-S3

Flash Firmware

  1. Connect your ESP32-S3 via USB
  2. Click the button below
  3. Select your device's serial port
  4. Wait for flashing to complete
Requires Chrome or Edge browser. Not supported on iOS.

After Flashing

Use the Keep CLI to interact with your device:

keep frost hardware ping --device /dev/ttyUSB0
